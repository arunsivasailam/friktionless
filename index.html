About Friktionless Labs

Who We Are

Friktionless Labs is a cutting-edge technology company operating at the intersection of space systems and life sciences. We specialize in Space Domain Awareness (SDA), Space Situational Awareness (SSA), advanced sensor systems, and biotechnology applications for both space and terrestrial environments.

Our Dual Focus

Space Technology: We lead in SDA/SSA solutions, providing comprehensive space object tracking, collision avoidance, and orbital safety systems. Our expertise spans electro-optical sensors, hyperspectral imaging, and real-time space monitoring platforms.

Life Sciences: We pioneer biotechnology solutions for space exploration and terrestrial applications, including space biology research, biomedical instrumentation, and astrobiology mission support. Our cross-domain expertise enables unique innovations at the frontier of space and life.
