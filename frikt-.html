About Friktionless Labs

Who We Are

Friktionless Labs is a cutting-edge space technology company specializing in advanced sensor systems and space-qualified product engineering. Founded by aerospace veterans and technology innovators, we bring decades of combined experience in satellite systems, optical engineering, and mission operations.

Our Mission

To democratize access to space technology by delivering innovative, reliable, and cost-effective solutions that enable the next generation of space missions. We're committed to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in electro-optical sensing and spacecraft engineering.

Technology Expertise

Optical Systems CubeSat Platforms Signal Processing Thermal Management FPGA Development Space Robotics RF Systems Power Electronics

Why Choose Us

Heritage & Innovation: We combine proven spaceflight heritage with cutting-edge innovation to deliver solutions that work in the harshest environment known to humanity.

Agile Development: Our streamlined processes enable rapid iteration and faster time-to-orbit without compromising quality or reliability.

Full-Spectrum Support: From initial concept studies to on-orbit operations, we provide comprehensive support throughout your mission lifecycle.

Quality Assurance: Every system undergoes rigorous testing in our state-of-the-art facilities, including thermal-vacuum chambers, vibration testing, and radiation exposure simulation.

Cost-Effective Solutions: We leverage commercial-off-the-shelf components and modular design principles to reduce costs while maintaining space-grade performance.

Let's Build the Future Together

Ready to take your space mission to the next level? Our team of experts is here to help you turn your vision into reality. Whether you need a complete system solution or specialized consultancy, we're ready to launch.
